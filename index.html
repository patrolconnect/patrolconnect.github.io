Introducing a dynamic web application streamlining the daily operations of Missouri officers and administrators.

Patrol Connect offers a wide array of essential functions,
simplifying various aspects of law enforcement work.

Search Warrant Generation

Empowering officers to efficiently create accurate search warrant applications with ease, ensuring legal compliance and saving valuable time.

Arrest Warrant Generation

Simplifies the process of generating arrest warrants, reducing paperwork and increasing the speed of legal proceedings.

Case Law Research

Provides access to an extensive database of case law, enabling quick and thorough research to support legal actions and decision-making.

Online Training Opportunities

Offers a range of external resources, both paid and free online training modules, allowing law enforcement professionals to enhance their skills and stay up-to-date with the latest developments in the field.

In-Person Training Opportunities

Facilitates the coordination of in-person training sessions, fostering continuous professional development for officers and administrators. Quickly provides access to several external outlets for obtaining professional development training.

License Scanning

The ability to scan a driver's license barcode to autofill information required in documents, saving time and ensuring accuracy.
