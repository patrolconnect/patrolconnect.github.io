A Modern Toolkit for Modern Policing

Explore the integrated modules designed to meet the specific operational needs of Missouri law enforcement.

Intelligent Warrant Generation

Construct court-ready search warrant applications and probable cause affidavits with unmatched speed. Utilize county-specific templates, save drafts as reusable templates for common cases, and let our AI-powered PC Review Assistant analyze your narrative to identify gaps and strengthen your statement before submission.

Automated Arrest Paperwork

Drastically reduce manual data entry for PC statements. Use your device's camera to scan a driver's license, instantly populating suspect information. The system automatically determines if the case is a felony or misdemeanor, selecting the correct document template for you.

Streamlined Digital Booking

Digitize your entire booking process. Complete and transmit dynamic, county-specific intake paperwork—including guided medical assessments—remotely from your vehicle. Minimize drop-off times and get back in service faster.

AI Report Analysis

Elevate the quality of your investigative narratives. Our AI tool provides legally-focused feedback to strengthen your reports for court. Our privacy-first approach automatically redacts PII on your device before analysis, ensuring sensitive data never leaves your control.

Actionable Crime Heatmapping

Turn raw data into tactical intelligence. Visualize crime incidents with a weighted heatmap and interactive marker clusters. Utilize advanced search and filtering to identify trends, generate leads, and create detailed statistical reports for your agency.

Automated Tow Sheet Generation

Create and distribute state-compliant tow sheets in seconds. Automatically decode a VIN using the integrated NHTSA lookup to populate vehicle details, use presets for local tow companies, and email the final PDF directly to records clerks and tow operators.

On-Demand Legal Research

Access the legal information you need, when you need it. Instantly search Missouri statutes by keyword or charge code. Each statute links directly to a pre-filtered Google Scholar search, providing relevant case law to support your decisions in the field.

Centralized Resource Hub

Your digital briefing book. Access a curated library of universal law enforcement resources and department-specific documents, policies, and forms (PDF/DOCX). Mark your most-used files as favorites for instant access.

Instant VIN Decoding

Quickly decode any 17-digit VIN using the official NHTSA database to get accurate vehicle information. An optional audio playback feature reads the results aloud, allowing you to stay focused on your surroundings during a stop.

Cyber-Crime Intelligence Tools

Take the critical first step in digital investigations. Our integrated IP lookup tool identifies geolocation and network data for any IP address and allows you to generate a printable intelligence report with your own case notes appended.

Curated Training Directory

Discover your next training opportunity. We provide a continuously updated directory of free and paid law enforcement courses—both online and in-person—from reputable providers, saving you hours of searching.

Unyielding Security & CJIS-Ready Compliance

We understand that law enforcement data is sensitive. Security isn't just a feature; it's the foundation of Patrol Connect. Our platform is built to meet and exceed the stringent requirements of the FBI CJIS Security Policy.

End-to-End Encryption

Your information is shielded at all times. We protect data in transit using the modern TLS 1.3 protocol and secure sensitive data in our database with powerful AES-256 encryption, meeting federal standards for data protection.

Robust Authentication

Your account is protected against unauthorized access. Passwords are never stored in a readable format, thanks to the industry-standard BCrypt hashing algorithm. We also automatically block brute-force login attempts to keep your credentials safe.

Hardened Server & Application

Our infrastructure utilizes a strong Content-Security-Policy (CSP) and other security headers to prevent XSS and clickjacking, while all database queries use prepared statements to eliminate SQL injection vulnerabilities.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I have to pay for each application module?

No. A single subscription to Patrol Connect grants you complete access to every feature and module we offer. We believe in providing complete value without hidden costs or paywalls. The only optional cost is a one-time setup fee for agencies wishing to add a custom digital jail intake process.

Can we use our own department's warrant templates?

Absolutely. While we provide universal, court-tested templates, we understand the importance of departmental consistency. We can integrate your agency's specific forms for Search Warrants and PC Statements at no additional cost.

How does Patrol Connect integrate with our existing RMS?

Patrol Connect is designed as a powerful standalone "force multiplier" that complements your existing software. It provides essential tools and efficiencies that may not be available in your current Records Management System (RMS), filling operational gaps without requiring complex or costly integration.

Which jail facilities can I send digital paperwork to?

You can transmit booking paperwork to any facility that has been onboarded to the Patrol Connect network. We are continuously expanding our network of partner facilities. If your local jail is not yet listed, please contact us, and we can begin the simple onboarding process with them.
