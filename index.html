A Modern Toolkit for Modern Policing

Explore the integrated modules designed to meet the specific operational needs of Missouri law enforcement.

Search Warrant Generation

Construct court-ready search warrant affidavits and applications with speed and precision. Our guided process ensures all legal elements are included, validated, and ready to file, reducing the risk of errors and omissions.

Probable Cause Statement Generation

Craft clear, professional, and consistent probable cause statements every time. Our system helps you articulate the necessary facts swiftly, reducing errors and saving valuable time.

Digital Jail Intake & Booking

Digitize your booking process. Complete and transmit intake paperwork remotely from your patrol vehicle or office directly to the correctional facility, minimizing drop-off times and getting you back in service faster.

Report Feedback

Elevate the quality of your reports. Our feedback tool helps ensure reports contain all necessary details and reduces speculation, strengthening your case for court.

Inter-Agency Crime Heatmap

Turn raw data into actionable intelligence. Securely share and visualize crime data with partner agencies on an intuitive heatmap, helping you identify trends, generate leads, and foster collaboration.

Tow Sheet Generation

Create and print Missouri-compliant tow sheets directly from your patrol vehicle. Send them wirelessly to an in-car printer or email them directly to records clerks for seamless processing.

On-Demand Case Law & Statute Research

Access the legal information you need, when you need it. Instantly search Missouri statutes by keyword or statute number and get relevant case law to support your decisions in the field.

Centralized Digital Resource Hub

Access a curated library of essential law enforcement resources. Agencies can securely upload their own documents, policies, and forms (PDF/DOCX), ensuring every officer has instant access to the latest information.

Instant VIN Decoding with Audio Playback

Quickly decode any VIN to get crucial vehicle information. Our unique audio playback feature reads the results aloud, allowing you to stay focused on your surroundings while on patrol.

Essential Cyber-Crime Intelligence

Identify the owners of IP addresses with our integrated lookup tool, providing a critical first step in digital and cyber-crime investigations.

Curated Training Resources

Discover your next training opportunity. We provide a continuously updated directory of free and paid training courses—both online and in-person—from various providers, saving you the time of searching for quality professional development.
